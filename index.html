Google Developer student club
Estam university

Google Developer Student Clubs is a Google Developers program for individuals in university to create opportunities for them to learn and improve their skills in technology. This program is also setup to create a community whereby every member of the community can share ideas and improve each other by cooperating.


Join Us!

Purpose of GDSC

The DSC program is a grassroots channel through which Google provides development skills, mobile and web development skills for students, towards employability.

How can I be a member

To become a member of the GDSC is very simple but the aim of being a member is all that counts. To become a member of the GDSC ESTAM, click on the join us button above.

Benefits of GDSC

  • GDSC helps students breach the gap between theory and practice.
  • students get to learn soft skills e.g web development etc.
  • It creates a very condusive environment for pear to pear learning
  • It helps students develop a strong communication skill by giving them the opportunity to speak at events

Opportunities GDSC provide students with.

  • Grow their knowledge on developer technologies and more through peer to peer workshops and events.
  • Showcase their prototypes and solutions to their local community and industry leaders.
  • Gain relevant industry experience by solving problems for local organizations with technology based solutions.
  • Getting inspiration to become world-class developers and changemakers from sharing others' success stories.

    Who can become a member?

  • The clubs is open to any student, ranging from novice developers who are just starting, to advanced developers who want to further their skills.
